QR

What is QMOQUI?

Our partnering company, under Entire Marketing Group LLC,QMOQUI, has partnered with Detour Maps to create custom, quick response codes (QR), for personal, organization, or business use.

Why have QR codes? Quick Response Codes allow your on-the-go residents to connect with you through their mobile phone.All they would need to do is scan the QR code, which canbe located anywhere, and it will take them to where you want them to go

Simple QR codes are free and easy to use.

A simple QR code can reveal your business’ website, a video, or even connect to social media.

Need something more advanced?

Custom QR code using QR+ allows you to connect all of your business or personal information in one spot. Think of it this way, what if your customers could click on your promotional items like a business card or flyer and instantly in-teract with you? QR+ technology makes that possible by using Detour Mobile Pages.

Unlike basic QR codes that normally have one function, QR+ gives users a wide range of options to interact. Send an email, call a number, watch videos, share on social media and more.

Detour Mobile Pages

Has all of your business information such as phone number,address, social media and more in one location. By using QR+ by QMOQUI,you can place a QR code any-where,from flyers to window decals.

QR+ has the ability to go beyond QR codes. Digitized images can be scanned to reveal any information you would like to share.It’s a fun way to interact with your customers. With one scan, a resident can have all of your information available to them on their mobile device.

QR+ is offered to companies, and organizations, and businesses

Detour Maps also serves entrepreneurs with MyQR. This service offers the same features as QR+, but for per sonal use. Promote yourself by placing a QR code on business cards, flyers, banners, website, etc.
