Privacy Policy - Detour Maps

Detour Maps and its Clients respect the personal privacy of the end users of the Services (such as individual consumers). This Privacy Policy describes how Detour Maps uses the information of people who visit this website and how Detour Maps and its Clients use information relating to consumers and other end users of the Services.

Further, this Privacy Policy does not apply to Facebook1, Twitter, or any other third party sites through which the Services may be delivered. Passive Information Collection

As you navigate through the Website or the Detour Maps mobile site, App or use the Services, Detour Maps may collect certain information passively (that is, information may be gathered without you actively providing the information) using various technologies and means, such as navigational data collection and geolocation data. Like most website operators, Detour Maps, its advertisers and third party service providers may track IP addresses, use both session and persistent cookies, and assign Internet tags. Detour Maps also automatically gathers information about the areas you visit on the Website and the Detour Maps mobile site and or App and collects operational information about the technology you use, such as your browser, type of computer or mobile device, operating systems, Internet service provider and the domain names of the websites from which you linked to the Website or the Detour Maps mobile site or App and to which you go after viewing such sites. If you activate geolocation applications, Detour Maps may have access to and store your geolocation information related to the Services.

The Website , mobile site and App may contain electronic images known as web beacons—also referred to as single-pixel gifs—that permit Detour Maps and/or its Clients to count users who have visited for other related statistics—(e.g., recording the popularity of certain Website mobile site or app content and verifying system and server integrity).

The reason Detour Maps collects passive information is that it helps us understand which areas of the Website, mobile site or App are most popular, which areas may require improvement and what technologies are being used by our visitors. This information helps Detour Maps to update and improve services. Detour Maps also uses this information for security purposes, to detect and to block security breaches and to provide you with a safer online environment.

In addition to the foregoing, Detour Maps will have access to information regarding your registration with and participation in the Offers and loyalty programs offered by participating Clients for which, or by which, you are registered ("Orange Deals/ Dashboard Data"). Program Participation Data may be used by Detour Maps to keep track of Orange Deals that you have accrued with each Client Detour Maps works with, which may allow you to check all of those programs you are enrolled in on one page of the Website.
