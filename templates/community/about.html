Locally owned businesses are the backbone of any comminity's economy. Join DetourMaps and other locall businesses and organizations to support your community by joining The Local Evolution

This campaign encourages residents to shop local versus shopping at a corporate or chain store. REsources suggest that for each dollar you spend at an independent business in your community, three or more times goes back into the local economy compared to a dollar spent at retailer owned by a chain. This menas better school, security, resources, and an overall better community.


We encourage to mpower individuals to become one with their local communities. Togethr we can create a stronger economy, a more vibrant community, and better job opportunities for local residents.

Print


Detour's printed map is a way have to have everthing there is to know about the community in the palm of you hand. With 2 publications each year for each community and over 40,000 maps distributed you will be well informed with our printed maps. Inside the printed maps include a map of your community with different local business advertisements. The map pin points each business giving the user a better idea of where the business or resource is located in the community. We do not just have business locations on the map; we also have locations of parks, bus routes, schools, useful phone numbers, events and basically all public assets in the community. The printed map also is color coded so that residents may know which business is participating in the $10 Savings Card promotion

ABOUT US

HISTORY

Detour Communty Maps
originated from MAPS (a marketing and Advertising Platform of Services) which is a division of Entire Marketing Group LLC established in 2007. The division was created to help the development of different communities in Illinois. We strive on connecting residents with local businesses and organizations to improve the community by creating a SHOP LOCAL campaign.

VISION

We are commited to help neighborhoods and organizations of all industries, sizes and locations by personalizing the clients brandings and marketing materials. When you partner with us, we help you by using a practical and effective advertising platform, whether it is print advertisement or adding your community to our online directory. Our vision is to help local businesses succeed by giving them the opportunity to advertise collectively and share the cost.

IMPACT

As of now, the participating businesses are Berwyn, Cicero, Archer Heights, Brighton Park, Back of the Yards, West Law, Chicago Law, Washington Heights, Morgan Park, and Roseland. Local organizations called Special Service Areas are also partnered with us, such as SSA 3, SSA 10, SSA 39, and SSA 45. These Special Service Areas work within the participating communities helping local businesses with different resources to improve the shopping corridors and quality of life.
